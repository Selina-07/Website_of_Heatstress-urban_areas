🛡️ Radiation Shielding Physics

To eliminate Radiative Bias (which can add +8°C to readings), we use a structured design:

  • 1. White ASA Outer Plate: Reflects shortwave solar radiation.
  • 2. Matte Black Underside: Absorbs ground albedo reflection.
  • 3. Micro-Aspiration: 5V axial fan maintains constant airflow (±0.1°C accuracy).

4-Phase Deployment Roadmap

1

Mobile Profiling & Mapping

Don't guess—map first.

Mount low-cost MUHM nodes on transit fleets (buses/garbage trucks) to identify thermal hotspots at 1-second intervals before committing to permanent infra.

2

Permanent Sensor Mesh

Grid deployment.

Deploy stationary nodes at 50-meter intervals in heat pockets using LoRaWAN backhaul for secure, long-range telemetry.

3

Edge-Actuated Cooling

The intervention phase.

Install automated shade sails and high-pressure (1000 PSI) misting lines controlled by psychrometric algorithms.

4

Municipal Integration

Policy and planning.

Feed real-time data into city Heat Action Plans (HAPs) to adjust worker shifts and issue targeted block-level alerts.
